What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit depends on your preferences and the specific destination. Generally, the dry season (June to October) is ideal for wildlife viewing, while the green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes and fewer crowds. For specific recommendations, please refer to our destination guides or contact our travel experts.

What is included in the price?

Our safari packages typically include accommodations, meals as specified in the itinerary, park fees, guided game drives, and airport transfers. International flights, visa fees, travel insurance, and personal expenses are usually not included. Please check the specific trip details on our website for a complete list of inclusions.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the trip and how far in advance you cancel. We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance. You can find detailed information about our cancellation policy in our Terms & Conditions.

Is travel insurance required?

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all our trips. It should cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, evacuation, and loss of baggage. We can recommend reputable insurance providers. Please see our insurance page for more details.
