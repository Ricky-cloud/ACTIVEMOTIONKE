Helicopter Tour

Everest Base Camp

Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the Everest Base Camp with our exclusive helicopter tour. Experience the majestic Himalayas like never before, soaring above breathtaking landscapes and witnessing the world's highest peak from a unique perspective. This tour offers a blend of luxury and adventure, ensuring a comfortable and awe-inspiring experience.

3-Day Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival & Acclimatization

Arrive in Lukla (2,860m) and trek to Phakding (2,610m). This gentle start allows for acclimatization while offering stunning views of the Dudh Koshi River.

  • Scenic flight to Lukla
  • Trek through Sherpa villages
  • Overnight stay in a cozy teahouse
2

Day 2: Trek to Namche Bazaar

A challenging yet rewarding trek to the bustling hub of Namche Bazaar (3,440m). The trail crosses several suspension bridges, including the famous Hillary Bridge.

  • First glimpse of Mount Everest
  • Explore the Sherpa market
  • Visit the Sherpa Museum
3

Day 3: Everest View & Return

An early morning hike to the Everest View Hotel (3,880m) for panoramic views of Everest, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam, followed by a helicopter ride back to Kathmandu.

  • Sunrise over the Himalayas
  • Breakfast with a view
  • Thrilling helicopter return journey
