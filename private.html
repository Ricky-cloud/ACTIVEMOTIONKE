Local Insights & Plans for Aspen

Discover hidden gems and make the most of your trip with our curated suggestions.

Local Tips & Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Maroon Bells for breathtaking sunrise views, but book your shuttle in advance!
  • Explore the lesser-known Smuggler Mountain Trail for great views without the crowds.
  • Enjoy après-ski like a local at the Ajax Tavern at the base of Aspen Mountain.

Suggested 3-Day Plan

  • Day 1:Arrival and exploration of downtown Aspen, followed by dinner at a farm-to-table restaurant.
  • Day 2:Full day of skiing on Aspen Mountain, with a gourmet lunch on the slopes.
  • Day 3:Morning hike to the scenic Crater Lake, followed by souvenir shopping before departure.

Best Time to Visit

For the best skiing conditions, visit between December and February. For hiking and summer activities, June through September offers pleasant weather and stunning scenery. The fall colors in late September are spectacular.
